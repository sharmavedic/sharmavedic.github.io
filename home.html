Current work
One of the biggest challenges in multi agent systems currently is efficient communication and coordination between agents. But on the other side, the field of opinion dynamics have multiple results in how agents come to an agreement or sometimes create partitions. Opinion dynamics works rarely talk about the "why?" do agents behave the way they do. There may well be an important connection that can bridge the gap between the two fields and also solve the problems they face.
